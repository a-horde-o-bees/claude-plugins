# Design principles gap: "flag missing capabilities, don't silently substitute" not encoded as a principle or rule bullet

Fix Foundations has "A missing capability in a tool interface is fixed by adding the capability, not by documenting a workaround" — but that's about fixing, not about the moment of discovery. The missing discipline: when the approach you're about to take doesn't do what the user actually asked for, that's a missing capability — flag it immediately, don't reframe a workaround as equivalent. Could be a bullet under Fix Foundations or Confirm Shared Intent.
