# Design principles gap: autonomous execution preference not encoded — reduce alignment checkpoints, default to proceed-and-report over ask-and-wait

## Purpose

Confirm Shared Intent currently gates on ambiguity, multiple approaches, and deviations — all asking before acting. The counterbalance is missing: when the path is clear, proceed without pausing. Distinguish load-bearing gates (scoping, final decisions) from habitual ones (mid-phase approval). Could be a qualifying bullet under Confirm Shared Intent or a separate principle.
